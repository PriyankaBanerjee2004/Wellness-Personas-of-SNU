Project Overview

This project clusters students at Sister Nivedita University into wellness personas using features: eating_out_per_week, food_budget_per_meal_inr, sweet_tooth_level and weekly_hobby_hours. The resulting personas help canteen management, health clubs, and student affairs tailor programs.

  • Author: Priyanka Banerjee
  • Date: 01/11/2025
  • Method: KMeans Clustering
  • Evaluation: Silhouette Score, Davies–Bouldin Index
